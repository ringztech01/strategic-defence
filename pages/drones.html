DRONES

We provide access to worldwide state-of-the-art drones, renowned for their precision, versatility, and reliability.

TYPES AVAILABLE

Attack Drones (Unmanned Combat Aerial Vehicles – UCAV)

Attack drones, also known as UCAVs or combat drones, are designed specifically for military operations. Equipped with advanced avionics, sensors, and weapon systems, they can deliver precision strikes against enemy targets while reducing the need for ground personnel in high-risk zones.

  • Available in multiple sizes for tactical or strategic missions
  • Advanced avionics and sensors
  • Can carry missiles, bombs, and guided weapons
  • Precision strike capability
  • Reduced risk to ground personnel

Reconnaissance Drones (Surveillance Drones)

Reconnaissance drones are designed for intelligence gathering, surveillance, and reconnaissance missions.

Applications

  • Military monitoring of enemy movements
  • Border surveillance
  • Disaster response and rescue operations
  • Law enforcement operations and investigations

Kamikaze Drones (Loitering Munitions / Suicide Drones)

These drones are designed to destroy targets by self-destructing upon impact after identifying targets.

  • Autonomous targeting
  • Extended loiter time
  • Precision impact
  • Low detection signature
